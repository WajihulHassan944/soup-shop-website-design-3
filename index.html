Our Soup Story

Welcome to the world of post-powder soups.

Let’s start with a fact. Soups are meant to be liquids. Warm, slurpy, broth liquids that are just delicious! Unique in the way that it’s the only food in the world that satisfies your tummy and your soul! Somewhere along the way, soups changed from liquids to powder, becoming unnatural and tasteless.
We want to change that. That’s why we’ve launched Brothsmen - liquid soups with real vegetables - the way soups should be enjoyed!

The Time Factor

We believe that time is precious, and an awful lot of it goes into putting together meals. Brothsmen was born to give people more options to enjoy healthy, hearty and nutritious food without having to spend time making it themselves. We wanted to make soup that looked like soup (liquid!),tasted out of the world, and would be available in a convenient, ready to eat pack, so you could pour, heat and eat!

Brothsmen soups balance deliciousness in taste, with wholesome ingredients, minus the artificial preservatives. Revolutionary retort packaging preserves the goodness of our soups for an extended shelf life of 9 months, without any need for refrigeration.

We can’t tick off all the items on your to-do list, but at least now you don't have to worry about making soups anymore!

Our Soups

If you love wholesome, natural and a whole lot of goodness-packed soups, then there’s nothing better than Brothsmen soups. Super tasty and super convenient. Open, pour, heat and eat.

Creamy Mushroom Soup

Farm fresh mushrooms, caramelised and slow cooked with onion, garlic and thyme, blended with a dollop of cream to give you a deliciously velvety soup. Enjoy the flavours of earthy, wholesome ingredients and real mushrooms in every bite. Pair it with garlic bread and enjoy a yummy meal! 100% vegetarian.

Classic Tomato & Basil Soup

This classic favourite is made from plump tomatoes, fresh basil, sauteed garlic and cream, blended together in the goodness of olive oil. Tangy and flavourful, this hearty comfort soup comes with an Italian twist. Add toasted croutons and you’ve got yourself a delicious meal! 100% vegetarian.

Tom Kha Soup

Made with fresh Thai paste, carrots, baby corn and mushroom, aromatic lemon grass, Kaffir lime, galangal and Bird’s eye chilli. Topped with creamy coconut milk, this wholesome soup brings the very best of Thai flavours in every spoonful. Add in cooked noodles and you’ve got yourself a delicious meal! 100% vegetarian.
